# Drift

A Claude skill for perpetual futures and spot trading on Solana: Drift Protocol SDK, vaults, market making, and liquidation mechanics.

## What the skill does

- Guides Drift SDK setup for perp and spot trading with correct precision handling
- Covers order types, position management, margin calculations, and liquidation pricing
- Provides trading bot template with risk management and market making strategies
- Includes vault delegation, Jupiter swap integration, and event subscription patterns

## Why now

Drift is Solana's largest perpetual futures protocol. The SDK enables programmatic trading, vault management, and market making — key primitives for trading bots and AI agents.
